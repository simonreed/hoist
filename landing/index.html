hoist.

Expose localhost over HTTPS on your own domain. No account. No limits.

curl -fsSL https://hoist.sh | sh
01

Connect your domain

Authenticate with Cloudflare and set up your tunnel — one command does all three steps.

hoist init yourdomain.com
02

Expose a local port

Pick a subdomain and a port. hoist creates the DNS record and starts the tunnel.

hoist myapp 3000
03

Share the URL

Your app is live at https://myapp.yourdomain.com — HTTPS, no port forwarding, no firewall rules.
